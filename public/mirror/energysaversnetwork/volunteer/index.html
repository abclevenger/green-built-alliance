Volunteer to Weatherize Low-Income Households in Asheville & Buncombe County

¿Habla español?

Looking for a fun hands-on volunteer opportunity in Asheville and Buncombe County to make a real impact in your community? Help us lower the energy cost burden of limited-income families while reducing energy consumption countywide in the fight against climate change. You can learn new energy efficiency skills and enjoy meeting new people while increasing your “happiness” factor by doing some good hands-on work.

OUR WEEKLY VOLUNTEER PROGRAM

Weatherize Client Homes

Worksite volunteers perform energy-saving upgrades in groups Tuesday-Friday 11am-3pm for clients in and around Buncombe County. In addition to connecting with great people and helping to restore the climate you will learn helpful energy-saving measures like changing lightbulbs, weatherstripping doors, and insulating water heaters. And most importantly, you will be helping to alleviate the energy cost burden of our most vulnerable populations in WNC, your very own neighbors who typically have much higher energy costs in relation to their income (typically 10x greater).

ON YOUR OWN TIME

Outreach/Table/Fundraise

There are thousands of families in WNC in need of energy assistance and/or education. Our “outreach” volunteers help us connect with area residents/organizations by posting fliers in residential communities, educating the public about energy efficiency, and contacting organizations that can pass our information out to clients in need. You can also table at events, take part in scheduling or calling clients, and/or help with fundraising. Work on your own time whenever and however much you want.
